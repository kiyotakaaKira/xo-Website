About The Weeknd



    Full Name: Abel Makkonen Tesfaye

    Stage Name: The Weeknd

    Date of Birth: February 16, 1990

    Origin: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
    
    Ethnicity: Ethiopian (parents emigrated from Ethiopia)
    
    Genres: R&B, Pop, Hip-Hop, Alternative R&B


The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his distinctive sound blending R&B, pop, and alternative genres, he has dominated global charts. His albums, such as "Starboy", "After Hours", and "Dawn FM", showcase his range from dark ballads to dancefloor anthems.

Popular Hits

Blinding Lights

"Blinding Lights" by The Weeknd has achieved numerous records, most notably becoming the longest-running song on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, surpassing the previous record held by Imagine Dragons' "Radioactive". It also holds the record for the most weeks within the top 5, top 10, and top 40 of the Hot 100 chart.
